Nodes

HyphaMind Nodes are sovereign enclaves of compute, storage, and policy—built for consent-first collaboration, receipts by default, and safe-to-change systems. Each node keeps authority and auditability close to the work, while contributing to a mycelial network of shared intelligence.

What makes a Node

  • Consent-first edges. Access is invited, explicitly scoped, and revocable.
  • Receipts by default. Every action leaves a verifiable trail you can read and share.
  • Reversible by design. Small, replaceable parts; rollbacks and unshare are first‑class.
  • Interoperate without lock‑in. Standards-aligned; truth traces its roots.

Emerging Node Examples

Steward Node

Governance, policy, and receipts coordination. Issue and verify attestations; manage consent and revocation across edges.

Research Node

Privacy-preserving data composting. Turn risky raw inputs into provenance‑linked artifacts for reproducible, reversible analysis.

Community Node

Consent‑first collaboration hub. Curate shared models and datasets with clear boundaries, receipts, and the right to revoke.
